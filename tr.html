Return of the Tomb Raider

Estimated Project Time: 3 months


Objective: To engineer the user interface, and be a level designer. I programmed the inventory system and special effects, as well as blocked out the Graveyard Level.

Outcome: I accomplished more than the stated objectives; I improved various levels such as the Graveyard, and also communicated with my team about unclear functionality that may prevent it from operating properly.

Insights: This was the first project I started learning about SCRUM reports. Being a fast learner, I learned how important they were to fostering team communication. Learning new skills in Unreal Engine quickly played a key role in my success as an engineer here.


Skills Learned:

  • Blueprints

  • Level Design

  • SCRUM Reports


Skills Applied:

  • Unreal Engine

  • Teamwork

  • Time Management

  • Attention to Detail
